Attractions in Madrid

Royale Palace of Madrid

Architectural style:Classical, Classicism, Baroque
Construction started:1734
The Palace is the offical residence of the Royal Spanish Family. It was created by the three architects Francesco Sabatini, Filippo Juvarra, José Segundo de Lema, Giambattista Sacchetti.

Santiago Bernabéu Stadium

Capacity:85,454
Team:Real Madrid C.F.
Estadio Santiago Bernabéu is a football stadium, housing the matches for Real Madrid. It was inaugurated the 14 December of 1947 and has there has been played more than 1,000 matches at the stadium.

Museo Nacional Del Prado

Function:Museum
Architectural style:Neoclassical architecture
Museo del Prado is the largest spanish museum of art. Located in the center of Madrid, it features the finest collection of European art, dating from the 12th century to the 19th century.
